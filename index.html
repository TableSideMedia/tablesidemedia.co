FAQ

Common questions, answered.

What types of restaurants do you work with? +

We work with restaurants of all sizes across the Chicago area. from neighborhood gems to established favorites. If you serve great food and want authentic exposure, we'd love to hear from you.

What does a restaurant need to provide? +

Restaurants provide a hosted meal for the creator during their visit. That's typically it. the creator handles everything else, and we coordinate the whole process on your behalf.

How are creators selected and matched? +

We carefully review each creator's content quality, engagement, and audience demographics. We then match them to restaurants based on cuisine style, aesthetic fit, and target audience alignment. so the pairing feels natural, not forced.

What does it cost for restaurants? +

Restaurants provide a complimentary meal for the creator. Beyond that, our pricing varies based on campaign scope. Fill out our inquiry form and we'll get back to you with a customized quote.

What content do creators post? +

Creators produce Instagram Reels and TikTok videos showcasing their dining experience. Content is authentic to their style. your restaurant is the subject, but the creator's voice drives the story.

How do I join as a creator? +

Fill out our creator application with your social handles and a bit about your content style. We review applications regularly and reach out to creators who are a strong fit for our restaurant partners.
